Born from a Love of Tabletop

GameBox started in a small garage in 2020 by three friends who were tired of spending hours scrolling through game reviews only to end up with a dud. We wanted to create a service that did the heavy lifting for you.

Today, we are a team of 20+ game enthusiasts, designers, and curators who play-test every single game before it even makes it onto our shortlist.
