Common Questions

Can I skip a month?
Yes! Life happens. You can skip any month from your dashboard before the 5th of the month.
When does my box ship?
All boxes ship on the 10th of every month. Delivery usually takes 3-5 business days depending on your location.
What if I don't like the game?
We offer a "Happiness Guarantee". If you don't like a game, we'll give you store credit for your next purchase or let you swap it in your next box.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings.
Are the games for all ages?
We have different curator profiles. You can choose to receive family-friendly games or more complex titles.
